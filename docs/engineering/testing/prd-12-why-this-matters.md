# PRD 12 — Why This Matters Testing

## Coverage Focus
- Anchor extraction validity and rejection of weak subjects.
- Event-type routing across funding, IPO, governance, policy, legal, macro, defense, product, and non-signal stories.
- Low-confidence fallback behavior for thin evidence.
- Batch-level repetition control and clause cleanup.
- Signal-label calibration for weak, moderate, and strong evidence.

## Validation Commands
- `npm install`
- `npm run lint`
- `npm run test`
- `npm run build`

## Human Review Focus
- Confirm explanations open with the right subject or a safe event phrase.
- Confirm unrelated domains do not leak into company, governance, or defense stories.
- Confirm low-data stories remain useful without sounding generic or malformed.
- Confirm signal labels feel proportionate to evidence quality.
